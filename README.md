# 📊 FinSight Financial Transactions Analytics Dashboard (2023–2026)

A Power BI dashboard project designed to analyze financial transactions, revenue generation, fees, taxes, and customer behavior across multiple years. The dashboard provides business stakeholders with a comprehensive view of transaction performance, customer segments, operational efficiency, and revenue trends to support strategic decision-making.

---

# Project Title

**FinSight Financial Transactions Analytics Dashboard | Power BI**

---

# Project Overview

FinSight is a Business Intelligence solution developed in Power BI to monitor and analyze financial transactions from 2023 to 2026.

The dashboard consolidates transaction activity, revenue streams, fees, taxes, customer demographics, and transaction success rates into a single reporting platform.

The solution consists of **two interactive dashboard pages**:

### Page 1 — Executive Overview
Provides a high-level view of transaction performance, revenue metrics, customer segmentation, and operational KPIs.

### Page 2 — Transaction Details
Offers detailed transaction-level insights for auditing, operational monitoring, and customer behavior analysis.

The dashboard enables management teams to monitor financial performance, identify business opportunities, and improve operational efficiency through data-driven insights.

---

# Business Problem

Financial institutions process thousands of transactions daily across different customer segments, transaction types, and geographic regions.

Without a centralized analytics platform, organizations face challenges such as:

- Limited visibility into transaction performance
- Difficulty tracking revenue generated from fees and taxes
- Inability to identify high-value customer segments
- Challenges monitoring transaction success rates
- Lack of insights into regional transaction activity
- Inefficient reporting processes

Management requires a solution that answers critical business questions:

- How much revenue is generated through transactions?
- Which customer segments drive the most activity?
- What are the most frequently used transaction types?
- Which regions generate the highest transaction volumes?
- What percentage of transactions fail or remain pending?
- How do fees and taxes contribute to overall revenue?

This dashboard was developed to provide actionable insights that support operational and strategic decision-making.

# Key Findings

## 1. Business Processed Over $125M in Transaction Value

The organization generated:

- **$125.44 Million** in transaction volume
- **14.94 Thousand** transactions

### Insight

The platform demonstrates strong transaction activity and revenue generation capability across multiple customer segments.

---

## 2. Average Transaction Value Remains High

Average transaction size reached:

> **$8.39K per transaction**

### Insight

Customers are conducting relatively high-value transactions, indicating a strong revenue base and potential focus on premium financial services.

---

## 3. Retail Customers Drive Most Transactions

Customer segment analysis reveals:

| Segment | Transactions |
|----------|-------------|
| Retail | 8.1K |
| Premium | 2.8K |
| SME | 2.4K |
| Corporate | 1.0K |
| Wealth | 0.6K |

### Insight

Retail customers account for the majority of transaction activity and remain the primary revenue driver.

---

## 4. Transaction Success Rate Exceeds 85%

Transaction status distribution shows:

| Status | Transactions |
|----------|-------------|
| Success | 12.83K |
| Failed | 1.51K |
| Pending | 0.60K |

### Insight

The system maintains a strong operational performance with a transaction success rate above 85%.

---

## 5. Maharashtra Generates the Highest Transaction Volume

Top-performing states include:

- Maharashtra
- Karnataka
- Gujarat
- Tamil Nadu
- Uttar Pradesh

### Insight

Transaction activity is concentrated in a few key regions, presenting opportunities for regional growth strategies.

---

## 6. Loan EMI and Transfers Generate Significant Value

Transaction type analysis indicates strong contribution from:

- Loan EMI
- Transfer
- Deposit
- Investment

### Insight

Loan-related and fund transfer services represent major business revenue streams.

---

## 7. Transaction Activity is Balanced Across Gender

Gender analysis shows:

| Gender | Transactions |
|----------|-------------|
| Female | 51.99% |
| Male | 48.01% |

### Insight

The customer base demonstrates balanced engagement across genders, reducing dependency on a single demographic group.

---

## 8. Fees and Taxes Contribute Additional Revenue

Revenue generated through operational charges:

- Total Fees: **$199.10K**
- Total Tax: **$35K**

### Insight

Ancillary revenue streams provide additional financial value beyond transaction processing.

# Actionable Recommendations

## Recommendation 1: Strengthen Retail Customer Retention

### Why?

Retail customers account for more than half of all transaction activity.

### Action

- Launch loyalty programs
- Offer personalized financial products
- Improve customer engagement campaigns

### Expected Impact

Higher customer retention and transaction frequency.

---

## Recommendation 2: Reduce Failed Transactions

### Why?

Approximately 10% of transactions fail.

### Action

- Investigate failure causes
- Improve payment infrastructure
- Implement automated error alerts

### Expected Impact

Increased transaction completion rates and customer satisfaction.

---

## Recommendation 3: Expand Premium & Wealth Segments

### Why?

These segments generate high-value transactions despite lower transaction volume.

### Action

- Introduce premium banking services
- Develop exclusive financial products
- Offer relationship management programs

### Expected Impact

Higher revenue per customer.

---

## Recommendation 4: Focus Growth Strategies on High-Performing States

### Why?

Transaction activity is concentrated in a limited number of regions.

### Action

- Increase marketing efforts in top-performing states
- Expand branch or digital presence
- Develop region-specific campaigns

### Expected Impact

Improved market penetration and customer acquisition.

---

## Recommendation 5: Optimize Revenue from Fees

### Why?

Fees generated nearly $200K in revenue.

### Action

- Review fee structures
- Introduce premium service tiers
- Bundle value-added services

### Expected Impact

Increased non-interest revenue streams.

---

## Recommendation 6: Promote High-Value Transaction Products

### Why?

Loan EMI, Investments, and Transfers contribute significantly to transaction value.

### Action

- Prioritize these products in marketing campaigns
- Offer incentives for adoption
- Improve customer onboarding

### Expected Impact

Growth in transaction value and profitability.
